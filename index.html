Sonish Sivarajkumar is a Ph.D. student in Intelligent Systems at University of Pittsburgh, supervised by Dr. Yanshan Wang. His research focuses on designing and validating advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning techniques for clinical information retrieval and extraction. His research interests include NLP, information retrieval, information extraction, and applications of these topics for clinical discoveries and improving healthcare. He is also interested in generative AI and foundational models that can create novel patient embeddings.

Sonish is an experienced professional in the field of healthcare and pharmaceutical industry, with a focus on AI technologies. With extensive industrial experience, he has established himself as an expert in the development of AI technologies in the healthcare industry. His experience includes roles at IQVIA, Genentech (Roche Pharmaceutical), and Merck, where he has contributed to various aspects of AI application in healthcare, from drug discovery to clinical development and patient care analysis.
